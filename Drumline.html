Drumline

The Drumline is one of the most important parts of the band because they help give us the marching time/tempo. The Drumline consists of 8 memebers. 2 marching snares, 2 marching quads and 4 bass drums. The Drumline this year has one of the most hardest parts of music, as they have many really fast rhythms that they need to play.
