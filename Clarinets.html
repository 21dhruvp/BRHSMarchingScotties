Clarinets

The clarinet section is The opposite of the flute section, in that it is a very big section. The clarinets get a lot of section sound and though they have a lot of members, their sound blends in well with the rest of the band. The only instrument in this section is the clarinet, for now. This section handles the other half of the higher pitch and range of the band. They can also however handle a little bit of the low parts, but not too low.
