Pit

The Pit is a very large part of the band as well. The pit is where all of the auxiliary percussion and mallet percussion is. Auxiliary percussion is the group of miscellaneous percussion instruments are, such as the tambourine, cymbals and breakdrum. The Mallet Percussion is where the big percussion instruments are, such as the vibraphone, xylophone and marimba.
