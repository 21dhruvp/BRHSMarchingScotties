The BRHS Marching Scotties are a Marching Band From Bordentown Regional High School. The Marching Scotties are under the Direction of Michael Montalto and are approaching 59 members of the band for the coming season. The Scotties are a Group 3A band this year and have a lot of competition. This year's show is titles "Chicago 1930" by Gary P. Gilroy. The Marching Scotties did amazing last season, so we will see if they can do just as good, if not better. The marching band has many diferent sections, which include...

  • Flutes
  • Clarinets
  • Saxophones
  • Brass
  • Drumline
  • and Pit
