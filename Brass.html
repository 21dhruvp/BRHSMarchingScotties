Brass

The Brass section, like the clarinet section, is a very big section that consists of trumpets, trombone and tuba. The brass section is a very balanced section. Even though, the only low brass is the trombone and tuba, the baritones help balance the sections out a lot. The brass section is very abrasive when there are loud sections, but can be quite quiet.
