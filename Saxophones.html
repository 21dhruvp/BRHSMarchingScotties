Saxophones

The alto saxophone section is a small but powerful section. They have lots of sound, though they only have 6 people. The tenor and baritone sections are the smallest section with 5 people. However, Their sound is also quite loud and ends up blending very well with the low brass and band. They all have a record of memorizing the music over summer break and coming into band camp ready to start drill.
